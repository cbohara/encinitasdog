Services & Pricing

Neighborhood Walks

I would love to take your dog for a 30 minute, 45 minute, or hour long walk in your neighborhood. A 30 minute walk is perfect for dogs with a medium energy level who need a little mid-day exercise. A 45 minute walk is great for dogs who have above average energy and take a little longer to tire out or overweight dogs who need to work off a pound or two. The hour long walk is recommended for very high energy dogs who need the extra exercise to tire them out.

30 minute walk: $20

45 minute walk: $30

60 minute walk: $40

Run/Hike in the Manchester Preserve Canyon

Just a mile east of Interstate 5 in Encinitas lies the Manchester Preserve, a 123-acre open space that offers several miles of trails through coastal sage scrub and chaparral. This is a great place for me to bring your athletic dog to run and hike so they can get the proper amount of daily exercise. A 30 minute, 45 minute, or hour long session is a great source of exercise for your pet and is sure to send them home tired and happy.

30 minute run: $40

45 minute run: $50

60 minute run: $60
